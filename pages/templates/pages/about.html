Our Company at a Glance

Carlos Gonzalez - Owner
Here at Gonzalez Welding Mobile, we are dedicated to you and your welding needs. I have been welding since 1993 for various companies and have worked side jobs involving commercial and residential projects. I provided superior service to my customers and I am currently certified to weld Stick, Mig and Tig (Mild steel, Stainless Steel and Aluminum) and also certified with D1.1, D1.5 wire NR-232 and core8, Dual Shield, E7018, Pipe 6G and ER5356 and more procedures.

Brief Work History

1993-2000

Bay Ship & Yacht, started as a labor and soon after started welding. My positions were production welding and a lead-man. I lead other welders in carrying out their job, which involved supervising and assuring that the work was done right.

2000-2001

Masters Tug and Tow, Junior Deck Engineer, my work included maintaining tugs structurally and mechanical while assisting on the deck.

2001-2005

Tutor Saliva, Production Welder "Journeyman", welded new columns, cofferdams, wailer systems, sheet piles, pipe piles and beam piles.

2005-2012

Salt River Construction, Lead Welder and Foreman, was in charge of all repairs, modifications and maintenance of equipment which includes: boats, barges, excavators and tractors.

2012 - Present

Mobile Rig Welder of my current company Gonzalez Welding Mobile. I am in charge of all projects and provide great service for all customers.
